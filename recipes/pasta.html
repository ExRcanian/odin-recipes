Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This is a recipe for a classic Spaghetti Carbonara, one of the simplest and tastiest pasta dishes youcan make.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Grated Pecorino Romano Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • 1/4 Pound of Guanciale or Alternative Fatty Pork Cut Like Bacon
  • 1 Pound Uncooked Pasta of Choice

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and salt liberally, then add in your pasta.
  2. Whisk together the eggs and cheeses and season with salt and pepper
  3. Heat up olive oil in a pan and add in your pork cut, then sautee until the fat is rendered and the meat begins to crisp
  4. Remove pasta and place into large mixing bowl along with one cup of pasta water, then mix in the cheese mixture and pork.
  5. Grate additional cheese to taste and serve!

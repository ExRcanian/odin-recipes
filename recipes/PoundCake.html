Poundcake

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious and moist pound cake.

Ingredients

  • 5 Eggs
  • 3 Sticks of Butter
  • 3 Cups of Sugar
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 3 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • Olive Oil

Directions

  1. Preheat an oven to 325F and use butter to grease a baking pan
  2. In a stand mixer, beat the butter until it is fluffy then gradually add in the sugar
  3. Add in the eggs and vanilla extract and mix until fully combined
  4. Pour the mixture into the baking pan and bake for 1 hour.
  5. Remove from the oven and allow the cake to cool and enjoy!

Protein Mug Cake

Description

This is a recipe for a 1-minute microwave mug cake that gives you all the protein you need to build muscle.

Ingredients

  • 430g or 1 Scoop of Whey Protein Powder of Choice
  • 1 Tbsp of Flour
  • 1/2 Tsp of Baking Powder
  • 1 Tbsp of Cocoa Powder
  • 1 Egg
  • 100 ml of Milk of Choice
  • Semisweet Chocolate Chips (Optional)

Directions

  1. In a large mug, mix together the protein powder, flour, baking powder and cocoa powder
  2. In a separate bowl or mug, mix together one egg and 100 ml of milk.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients in a single mug and optionally mix in some chocolate chips.
  4. Place mug in microwave and microwave for 1 minute or until desired temperature is reached.
  5. Remove from microwave, allow to cool and enjoy!
